About John Sauber

Real estate isn't just my career — it's my way of helping families build the life they've always dreamed of.

Your Agent

Helping Families Find Home

I'm John Sauber, a licensed real estate agent based in Apple Valley, Minnesota. I founded Sauber Real Estate with a simple mission: to make buying and selling a home feel less like a transaction and more like a partnership.

I grew up in the south metro and know these communities inside and out — the best neighborhoods for young families, the hidden-gem properties that don't last long, the school districts that parents rave about, and the local spots that make each town special.

Whether you're a first-time buyer nervous about making the biggest purchase of your life, or an experienced homeowner looking to upgrade, downsize, or invest — I bring the same level of dedication, honesty, and energy to every client.
