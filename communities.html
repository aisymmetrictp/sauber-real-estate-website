Apple Valley

The Heart of Dakota County

Featured Community

Apple Valley

Apple Valley is one of the most sought-after family communities in the south metro. Home to the award-winning ISD 196 school district, the Minnesota Zoo, and miles of trails, it offers the perfect balance of suburban convenience and outdoor recreation.

Whether you're looking for a cozy starter home or a spacious four-bedroom, Apple Valley has options for every budget. The community pride here is something special — festivals, farmers markets, and friendly neighbors make it easy to feel at home.

Lakeville

Where Families Flourish

Growing Community

Lakeville

Lakeville is one of Minnesota's fastest-growing cities, and for good reason. New construction, excellent schools (ISD 194), and a charming downtown make it a magnet for young families and professionals alike.

From lakeside parks to the bustling Heritage Center, Lakeville offers a community where kids can play, parents can connect, and everyone feels welcome. The range of housing — from affordable townhomes to luxury estates — means there's a place for every family.

Eagan

Urban Convenience, Suburban Heart

Established Community

Eagan

Eagan offers the best of both worlds — easy access to Minneapolis/St. Paul with the warmth and safety of a close-knit suburban community. Home to the Twin Cities Premium Outlets, Lebanon Hills Regional Park, and excellent dining, Eagan keeps families entertained and connected.

The housing stock ranges from well-maintained ramblers and split-levels to newer townhomes and upscale properties. It's a fantastic option for families who want space without sacrificing convenience.
