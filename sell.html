Questions

Seller FAQs

How do you determine the right listing price? +

I prepare a detailed Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) that examines recent sales of similar homes in your area, current competition, market trends, and your home's unique features. This data-driven approach ensures we price your home to attract serious buyers while maximizing your return.

What do I need to do to prepare my home for sale? +

I'll provide a personalized checklist based on a walkthrough of your home. Generally, decluttering, deep cleaning, and minor repairs make the biggest impact. I can also recommend trusted stagers, handymen, and cleaners to help get your home show-ready.

How long will it take to sell my home? +

In the current market, well-priced homes in the south metro are selling within 2-4 weeks on average. However, timing depends on location, price point, condition, and season. I'll give you a realistic timeline based on current market conditions for your specific area.

What are the costs of selling a home? +

Typical seller costs in Minnesota include agent commissions, title insurance, state deed tax, and closing fees. I'll provide a detailed net sheet so you know exactly what to expect. There are no surprises — transparency is a core part of how I work.
